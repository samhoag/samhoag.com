WELCOME TO THE FUTURE!

Do you know where you want to live in the future? As a matter of fact, do you know which places around the country will even be habitable?

This webpage plans to help answer all your questions! Of course the news and other sources of information will tell you all of the harmful effects of climate change and the solutions that we should be put in place. However, they never tell you if a state or even just a city, is at risk of being inhabitable.
